Thermoelectric AC Prototype

A solid-state cooling prototype using Peltier (TEC) modules to eliminate conventional refrigerants.

Goals

  • Reduce reliance on vapor-compression systems
  • Evaluate COP vs. airflow design
  • Prototype compact desktop cooling unit

Key Components

  • TEC1-12706
  • Aluminum heat sinks
  • Thermal paste
  • PWM fan control
  • 12V DC supply

Progress

Achieved a 14–16°C delta across the hot/cold sides under stable load with optimized airflow baffles.

Confetti Launcher Build

Spring & compressed-air hybrid launcher designed for safe indoor celebration effects.

Design Highlights

  • Removable cardboard barrel sleeves
  • Fast-reset plunger system
  • Low-mass biodegradable paper fill

Safety Considerations

Edges rounded, pressure limited via bleed ports, no metal fasteners in discharge path.
